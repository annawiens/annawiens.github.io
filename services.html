Private Practice

1) Individualized Early Intervention - This is what I call “purposeful parenting”!! Learn how you can facilitate each milestone of your baby’s development from day one.

  • Feeding - Breast and bottle feeding, to solids introduction, through texture progression and eventually an age appropriate toddler diet.
  • Development – Sleep and regulation, gross motor and fine motor milestones.

2) Developmental Play Groups – 60 to 90 minute developmental play groups, for 2-4 babies and their caregivers.

  • Babies get the benefit of watching and learning from each other and practicing early social skills. I will facilitate each individual baby’s next developmental goals, and provide handouts and recommendations for purposeful play to continue at home.
  • Caregivers bond and support each other through shared experience.

Location

I reside in SE Calgary with my husband and three kids.

  • In person sessions in your home and neighborhood if distance allows.
  • Plus virtual support through video sessions or phone consults when face to face is not feasible.
  • Remote consultation available to anywhere in Alberta, via video sessions.

Insurance Coverage

Occupational therapy is a benefit under most Alberta Blue Cross plans. Please review your specific coverage plan as more and more third party insurance providers are recognizing O.T. as a benefit.
